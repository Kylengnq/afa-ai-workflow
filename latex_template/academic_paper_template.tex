\section{Introduction}
\label{sec:intro}

Start with the research question and why it matters. Use \verb|\citet{}| for in-text citations (e.g., \citet{fama1993common}) and \verb|\citep{}| for parenthetical citations (e.g., \citep{petersen2009estimating}).

\section{Literature Review}
\label{sec:literature}

Organize by theme, not paper by paper. Identify strands of related work and synthesize what the field knows, where it disagrees, and what remains open.

\section{Discussion}
\label{sec:discussion}

Discuss implications, limitations, and directions for future research.

\section{Conclusion}
\label{sec:conclusion}
