\section{AI Workflow and Compliance Statement}
\label{app:workflow}

Narrative summary of how AI tools were used at each stage of the project:
idea generation, literature review, identification, data construction,
empirical analysis, robustness, writing, and revision. Note the stages that
were AI-driven, the stages that involved direct human work, and the points at
which the two interacted.

Also confirm the AFA eligibility conditions: the investigation began on or
after 2026-06-01, the initial prompt was the first project act, the project was
entirely new, all human work was performed by named authors, and each author
complied with the lead-author and total-submission caps. Pull this from
\texttt{submission/workflow.md},
\texttt{submission/initial\_prompt.md}, and
\texttt{submission/call\_requirements.md}.

\section{Initial Prompt and Model Configuration}
\label{app:prompt}

Reproduce the initial prompt verbatim, along with its issuance date, the

\section{Human Contribution Log}
\label{app:human}

Summary of human work on the project. Include the total hours by author, the
distribution of activities by stage, and the headline percentages from the
contribution report (code lines, writing words, documentation lines).
The full session-level table lives in \texttt{submission/human\_time\_log.md};
the percentages and method come from \texttt{submission/contribution\_report.md}.
Direct human contributions outside prompts should be named here rather than
left implicit.
